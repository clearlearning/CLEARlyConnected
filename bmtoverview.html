Board Member Training Programs

Board and council members take on a great responsibility, and they must be equipped for their roles. CLEAR's Board Member Training programs offer multiple levels of training, education, and support to help ensure board and council members understand their role and can serve effectively in the public interest.

So You Think You Want to Be a Board or Council Member?

This program is designed to provide prospective and newly elected or appointed regulatory board or council members with an introduction to the expectations and requirements of regulatory board leadership.

Covers four main aspects:

  • Regulation
  • Governance
  • Roles & Responsibilities
  • Ethics

Introduction to Regulatory Governance

Designed for regulatory board/council members, CLEAR's Online Introduction to Regulatory Governance program provides accessible and affordable training that equips members for their important public service role.

CLEAR provides four ways to access this program:

  • In-person
  • Online, self-paced
  • On-demand
  • CLEAR Webinar

Advanced Concepts in Regulatory Governance

This workshop is for seasoned regulatory board/council members and promotes strategies for dealing with specific challenges board members face.

In-Person Program: Two-day intensive workshop

Virtual Program: Comprehensive one-week class utilizing self-paced content and live online instructor-led sessions.
